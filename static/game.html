    Cocktail Menu & Scoring
    First match wins — reach 40 pts to win the game.
    How to Play
    Objective

    Be the first to sell 40 pts worth of drinks — or claim 3 karaoke cards for an instant win.

    Your Turn — choose one action
    • Take Ingredients — take from the display or bag
    • Sell a Cup — sell a valid drink for points
    • Drink a Cup — drink the full contents of a cup
    • Go for a Wee — empty bladder, sober up 1 level
    • Claim a Card — pay bladder cost, keep the card
    • Refresh a Row — discard & replace a card row (drunk ≥3 only)
    Take Ingredients

    Take exactly 3 + your drunk level ingredients per turn. Draw from the open display (visible) or blindly from the bag, in any order.

    You must decide what to do with each batch before drawing more. For every ingredient:

    • Cup — place in one of your cups (max 5 per cup)
    • Drink — goes to your bladder; adjusts drunk level at end of turn
    • Special token — roll the die: Bitters / Cointreau / Lemon / Sugar / Vermouth / Nothing. Specials go on your player mat (not in cups).
    Drunk level changes (applied once after all ingredients)
    • Drank any spirits → drunk level +1 per spirit (mixers have no effect)
    • Drank only mixers → drunk level −1 per mixer (minimum 0)
    • Drunk level > 5 → hospitalised, out of the game
    • Bladder overflows → you wet yourself, out of the game
    Sell a Cup

    Sell a valid combination of spirits and mixers. Ingredients return to the bag. See the Menu for cocktail recipes (10–15 pts), pairings, and simple drink scores.

    • Single spirit + valid mixer(s): 1 pt
    • 2 spirits (same) + valid mixer(s): 3 pts
    • Tequila Slammer (exactly 2×Tequila): 3 pts
    • Cocktail: 10 pts  |  Long Island Iced Tea: 15 pts

    Max 2 spirits for non-cocktail drinks. Cocktails require exactly the listed spirits, mixers, and declared specials — no extras.

    Go for a Wee

    Empty your bladder — all ingredients return to the bag. Sober up 1 level. Each wee uses one toilet token, shrinking bladder capacity by 1 (minimum 4). You may wee even with an empty bladder.

    Claim a Card

    Pay the card’s cost using ingredients currently in your bladder. Keep the card permanently for its special ability. A new card is drawn to replace it. If the deck is empty, the slot stays empty.

    Refresh a Row of Cards

    Only available when drunk ≥ 3. Discard all non-karaoke cards in a chosen row and replace them from the deck. Karaoke cards are never removed from the display.

    Drink a Cup

    Drink the entire contents of one of your cups. The same drunk and bladder rules apply as when taking ingredients.
